about Ideas4Life

LiFE is a global mass movement which utilises behaviour science to nudge individuals to adopt sustainable lifestyles and contribute to climate goals. Through simple behaviour nudges and changes in people's daily lives, LiFE aims to harness the accumulated climate impact of an individual's sustainable lifestyle choices. These nudges are as small as encouraging individuals to consume less plastic, waste less food, and prefer public transport.

About Meri LiFE

MeriLiFE empowers individuals to lead a sustainable lifestyle by making pro-planet choices in their daily lives. The app is inspired by the concept of LiFE, introduced by the Honourable Prime Minister at COP 26, which emphasizes mindful and deliberate utilization instead of mindless and wasteful consumption.

Meri LiFE is a marketplace where young people can explore their interests, find Climate Change and Environmental Sustainability focused opportunities that match their interests, and sign up to act on issues that matter the most to them. In this way, through Meri LiFE, YuWaah will thus activate young people as change makers in their communities, to lend their creativity, grit, and power to have social and environmental impact.

About Mission LiFE

Mission LiFE: An India-led global mass movement to nudge individual and community action to protect and preserve the environment.

Mission Lifestyle for Environment recognises that Indian culture and living traditions are inherently sustainable. The importance of conserving our precious natural resources and living in harmony with nature are emphasised in our ancient scriptures. The need of the hour is to tap into that ancient wisdom and spread the message to as many people as possible. Mission LiFE seeks to channel the efforts of individuals and communities into a global mass movement of positive behavioural change.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Ideas4Life Hackathon 2023?

The Ideas4Life Hackathon 2023 is an innovative tech event aimed at bringing together creative minds to collaborate and develop solutions using cutting-edge technology to address real-world challenges.

Who can participate?

Undergraduate Students of Engineering Colleges, University colleges, University campus colleges, affiliated Arts and Science, Agriculture, Veterinary science and Fisheries colleges of various universities.

  • UG students of select Deemed universities and autonomous colleges.
  • Students of Polytechnics, and Industrial Training Institutes (ITI).

    • These students form into 3-5 member teams to find solutions for the given local problems of their districts using technology in an innovative way.

    In such an innovation effort, the result found by the students is usually a newly developed marketable product. (prototype) It is expected that the local problems that are under pressure will be resolved.

    How do I register for the hackathon?

    Registration details will be available soon on the official website. Keep an eye on our social media channels for updates.

    Are there any prizes for winners?

    Yes,

  • First Prize - Rs.1 lakh each for top 20 teams
  • 2nd Prize Rs.25,000 each for 60 teams
  • Students at each level will be awarded Certificate of participation, Certificate of Appreciation, Certificates for Student Mentors, Certificates for Leaders, Certificates for Best Higher Education Institutions
    • Will there be mentors or workshops during the hackathon?

    Yes, trained mentors from your institution will be there throughout the event to guide and assist. Workshops and boot camps will be organised for selected teams.
